Adding Custom License Plates

Requirements

  • LÂ² License Plates
  • Easy Entity Inspector

Steps

Step 1 Get the template.

PLATE:RegisterPlate("VEHICLE_NAME", {
	pos = Vector(0, 0, 0),
	ang = angForward,
	scale = 1,
},{
	pos = Vector(0, 0, 0),
	ang = angBackward,
	scale = 1,
})
Step 2 Go to the Q Menu, right click the vehicle you want then "copy to clipboard".
Replace VEHICLE_NAME with your clipboard contents.
Step 3 Get out your Easy Entity Inspector Tool
Step 4 Click on the vehicle, where you want your front license plate.
Step 5 Find the second "Vector(" position, then click the "copy to clipboard" button.
Step 6 Replace the first vector in the template with the vector you copied.
Step 7 Adjust to liking.
Step 8 Repeat with the second vector, for the rear plate.
Custom Angles For minor adjustments, I'd suggest taking the angle (angForward / angBackward) and adding an angle to it.
Complete Example 

PLATE:RegisterPlate("chev_cucv_lw", {
	pos = Vector(0, 106.3, 37.2),
	ang = angForward + Angle(0, 0, 15),
	scale = 1,
},{
	pos = Vector( 0, -139.5, 31.5),
	ang = angBackward,
	scale = 1,
})
